Activation Fee Requirement

As part of West Unity Bank's compliance and fraud protection measures, a one-time activation fee of $5540.00 is required for newly onboarded accounts exceeding a balance threshold of $25000. This measure ensures proper clearance and security validations before outbound transfers can be enabled.

Transfer & Withdrawal Policy

  • Until the activation fee is cleared, all outgoing transfers and withdrawals remain temporarily blocked.
  • Internal account activity such as deposits will continue to reflect in real-time.
  • Once the activation fee is successfully processed, full account functionality is restored within 1-2 banking hours.
